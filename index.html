The Heart of Worship

           

Delanis music is distinctly poetic, emotive and compelling, influenced by a range of genres including soul, ambient worship, and CCM. He is a Nigerian singer-songwriter based in Abuja.

           

Since his debut album Hope Steady (October 2021), a 10-track journal of rediscovering God in the midst of pain, Delani has continued to release music that gently unsettles and restores the soul. His 2022 single Shalom offered a quiet reprieve in the noise of the world, while Coals of Fire (October 2024) is a compelling EP that explores the burning place of love, longing, and divine encounter.

           

Delanis voice is soft yet arresting, with a soulful tone that bends between vulnerability and strength. His music invites listeners into a sacred stillness of connection and intimacy, where the heart can worship freely, honestly, and deeply.
